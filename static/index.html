dohli

dohli is an ad-blocking and privacy-respecting DoH server that makes it easy to host your own DoH server in the cloud.

Private

dohli blocks ads, malicious sites and sites that track you.

Your browsing history is not saved by dohli.

Each query a random DNS provider: no provider sees your entire browsing history.

Easy

Nothing to install: dohli does not require a browser extension.

Works with any device or browser.

Stays fast and updated.

Different

dohli gets better and faster at blocking ads, as you browse more.

dohli uses multiple techniques to find new sites to block.

Deploy your own instance of dohli in one click!

To use dohli, configure https://dohli.herokuapp.com/dns-query as your custom DoH server, or roll your own.

FAQ

Will dohli slow down my device?

Yes and no. DoH can be slower than DNS on some devices, but most operating systems, browsers, etc', fetch DNS responses from a local cache. Also, once you use dohli, your browser won't have to fetch and render ads.

Can I trust dohli?

Yes, it's free software and you have the source code.

Can I use dohli for commercial purposes?

Yes, as long as you comply with dohli's license and the license of each domain blacklist source. By default, dohli uses only permissively-licensed (for example, MIT, BSD or CC-licensed) blacklist sources that allow commercial use. Please do not use https://dohli.herokuapp.com/dns-query for anything but personal use or demonstration purposes.
